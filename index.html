Technical Interview Prep

Preparing for technical interviews requires consistent practice and understanding of core concepts. I focused on arrays, strings, hash maps, and recursion to build a strong foundation.

The key is to not just memorize solutions, but to understand the patterns and be able to apply them to new problems.

Resources I Used

LeetCode was my primary resource for practice problems. I also watched YouTube tutorials and read articles about system design and coding best practices.

Consistency is more important than intensity. Even 30 minutes a day can make a huge difference over time.
